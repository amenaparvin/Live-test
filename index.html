Destinations in Asia

Kyoto, Japan

Description

Kyoto, the ancient capital of Japan, is renowned for its well-preserved temples, traditional wooden houses, and stunning gardens. Highlights include the Fushimi Inari Shrine with its iconic torii gates, the serene Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and the beautiful Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
Kyoto is also famous for its traditional tea ceremonies and the historic Gion district, where you might spot a geisha.
